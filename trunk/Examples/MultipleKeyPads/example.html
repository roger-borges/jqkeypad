Multiple KeyPad Demonstration

A few things to note:

  1. Clicking Caps will switch to the Caps Lock keyboard
  2. Clicking NumPad will switch to the NumberPad keyboard
  3. Clicking the Keyboard button on the Caps Lock or NumberPad keyboards will take you back to the original keyboard
  4. Pressing Space(SpaceCommand) will take the current text and add it to the textarea box with a space appended
  5. Pressing Enter will append the current text to the textarea and append a new line
